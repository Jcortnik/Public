Slide 9: Scenario 2 - Slurry Blends for Paints & Coatings

Key Content:

  • Strategy: Blend PCC slurry (60% solids) with Imerys minerals (DE/wollastonite); target architectural/industrial (4-5% CAGR).
  • Advantages: Enhanced opacity/durability; TiO2 extender for 10-15% cost savings.
  • Price: $500/MT (value-added blend); Margin: 40% ($200/MT profit).
  • At Max Cap: Revenue $6M; Profit $2.4M/year.

Visuals: Bar chart: Performance Metrics (Opacity +20%, Cost Savings 15% vs. standalone); revenue bar vs. base PCC.

Slide 10: Scenario 3 - Develop Calcium Citrate Product

Key Content:

  • Strategy: Convert PCC to calcium citrate (via citric acid reaction); grow in supplements/food (3-5% CAGR).
  • Advantages: Higher bioavailability than carbonate; enter $0.92B market.
  • Price: $1,200/MT (premium derivative); Margin: 75% ($900/MT profit).
  • At Max Cap: Revenue $14.4M; Profit $10.8M/year.

Visuals: Area chart: Market Penetration (Start 5% share in NA; grow to 15% over 5 years, revenue to $72M cumulative).
